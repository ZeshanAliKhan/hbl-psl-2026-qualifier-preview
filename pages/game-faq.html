HBL PSL 2026 Qualifier questions answered.

When is the HBL PSL 2026 Qualifier?

The Qualifier is scheduled for Tuesday, April 28, 2026.

What time does it start?

The listed start time is 7:00 PM PKT. That is 10:00 AM ET and 7:00 AM PT in the United States.

Where is the Qualifier being played?

The revised official schedule places the Qualifier in Karachi. The venue context used here is National Bank Stadium, Karachi.

Which teams will play?

The teams are still to be confirmed. The Qualifier is for the top two teams after the league stage is complete.

Does the loser get eliminated?

No. The Qualifier loser moves into Eliminator 2, while the winner goes directly to the final.

Why not name the teams now?

Because doing that before the league table is settled would be a guess. This page is built to stay accurate before the final top-two order is confirmed.
